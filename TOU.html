Threads of Unity

Join us to hand-stitch the longest UAE flag—a tapestry of our nation's pride, woven by the hands of its people.

Become a Part of History

One Nation, One Flag, One Heart.

"Threads of Unity" is a landmark national event celebrating the UAE's spirit. We are bringing together residents of all ages and backgrounds, with a special focus on the wisdom and skill of our elders, to achieve a monumental goal: hand-stitching the longest UAE flag ever created.

This initiative is more than an event; it's a social movement designed to connect generations, celebrate our shared home, and create a lasting symbol of national pride that will be remembered for years to come.

Be Part of a Living Symbol of Unity

Your participation is more than just a stitch—it's a story, a connection, and a legacy.

Experience Togetherness

Share a meaningful moment with people from all walks of life, connected by a common love for the UAE.

Stitch History

Your contribution becomes a permanent part of a monumental flag—a tribute to every individual's story.

Celebrate the Nation

Enjoy a vibrant day filled with community activities, art, and a shared sense of purpose and pride.

Receive a Keepsake

Each participant receives a commemorative certificate to remember this collective act of harmony.

In Collaboration With

This initiative is made possible by the heartfelt collaboration of two dedicated organizations.

Elder Square

The Hands That Build Our Nation

The talented and spirited residents of Elder Square are the heart of this project. Their hands, rich with life and experience, will be stitching every piece of this monumental flag. Elder Square provides a vibrant, revolutionary community for seniors to thrive, and their participation embodies the wisdom and legacy we aim to celebrate.

Be Kind Friends

The Spirit of Kindness

"Be Kind Friends" is the movement powering this initiative. As proud sponsors, their belief in kindness as a force for change is the thread that connects us all. They are dedicated to creating a legacy of compassion and shared humanity, making them the perfect partner to bring this vision of unity to life.
